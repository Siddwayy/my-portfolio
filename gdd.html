Pit2 - Game Design Document

Pit2 is a fast-paced, 2D multiplayer arcade sport that reimagines the classic Indian game Lagori (Pitthu) for the digital arena. Two teams of three players battle it out in a clash of precision, reflexes, and teamwork — one side striking to scatter the iconic stack of seven stones, the other racing to rebuild it under pressure. With quick-time mechanics driving every hit and rebuild, every moment counts. Designed for intense 3v3 matches, Pit2 blends traditional play with modern competitive energy — easy to pick up, hard to master, and endlessly replayable.
